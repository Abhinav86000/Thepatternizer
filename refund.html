Refund Policy

Fair refund rules for a digital product.

The Patternizer is a low-cost digital access product. This policy explains when refunds may be considered and how to contact support.

Last updated: 14 June 2026

1. Digital access product

The Patternizer provides digital access to data analysis features. There is no physical product, shipping, or manual installation.

2. Standard refund approach

Refunds may be considered in genuine cases such as duplicate payment, payment charged but access not activated, or a technical issue that prevents access and cannot be resolved after support review.

3. Non-refundable cases

Refunds may not be available when the user has successfully received access and used the product, changed their mind after purchase, uploaded unsuitable or poor-quality data, expected custom consulting work, or violated the Terms and Conditions.

4. How to request a refund

Email abhinav86000@gmail.com with your registered email address, payment date, payment screenshot or transaction reference, and a short explanation. Please allow up to 2 working days for a response.

5. Refund processing time

If a refund is approved, processing timelines may depend on Razorpay, the bank, card network, or payment provider. The final credit date is controlled by the payment ecosystem and may vary.

6. Cancellation

The current paid plan is lifetime access, not a recurring subscription. Therefore, there is no monthly cancellation. Users may stop using the service at any time.
