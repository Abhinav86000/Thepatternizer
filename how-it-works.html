How it works

Six simple steps from spreadsheet to decision.

The Patternizer is built for people who want answers, not complicated setup.

1. Upload data

Add your Excel, CSV, TSV, or text file.

2. Detect variables

The app detects string variables, numeric variables, and time columns.

3. Manage measures

Select the numeric measure you care about most, such as sales, profit, units, margin, cost, or score.

4. Find all signals

Get good things, opportunities, hidden clusters, and anomalies in one analysis flow.

5. Drill into details

Explore category summaries, relational charts, and statistical measures.

6. Use findings

Use the story to make decisions, prioritize actions, and reduce blind spots.
