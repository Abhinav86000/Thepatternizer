Statistical summary without statistical confusion.

Patternizer gives the important numbers in a simple way. These help you understand whether your numeric measure is stable, volatile, concentrated, or unusually spread out.

Standard DeviationShows how much values move away from the average. Useful for risk, stability, and spread analysis.
KurtosisShows whether the data has extreme peaks or heavy tails. Useful for spotting unusual concentration.
Coefficient of VariationCompares variation with the average. Useful when you want to compare consistency across measures.
SkewnessShows whether values lean more to one side. Useful for understanding imbalance in sales, margin, or volume.
