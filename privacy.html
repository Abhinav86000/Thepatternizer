Privacy Policy

Your data should stay understandable, useful, and respected.

This Privacy Policy explains how The Patternizer handles email login, uploaded files, payment information, and product usage. Contact: abhinav86000@gmail.com

Last updated: 14 June 2026

1. Information we collect

We may collect your email address when you sign in, your payment confirmation details when you purchase access, and basic technical information needed to operate the website securely. The Patternizer uses Supabase for authentication and access status, and Razorpay for payment processing.

2. Uploaded datasets

The Patternizer is designed to analyse user-uploaded Excel, CSV, TSV, and text files in the browser wherever possible. Uploaded data is used to generate summaries, charts, variable detection, statistical measures, good signals, opportunities, clusters, and anomaly insights. You should not upload highly sensitive, illegal, confidential, or regulated data unless you have the right to process it.

3. Authentication and access

Email login is used to identify whether a user has demo access or paid access. Your access status may be stored in a Supabase database using fields such as email, paid status, payment date, and payment reference.

4. Payments

Payments are processed by Razorpay. The Patternizer does not ask you to send card details by email. Payment confirmation may be used to activate paid access for your registered email account.

5. How information is used

  • To allow secure sign-in.
  • To verify free or paid access.
  • To provide data analysis features.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To prevent misuse, fraud, or unauthorized access.

6. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures for security. However, no website can guarantee absolute security. Users are responsible for uploading only data they are allowed to analyse.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, email abhinav86000@gmail.com. Response time may be up to 2 working days.
