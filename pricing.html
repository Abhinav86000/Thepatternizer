What “lifetime access” means

Lifetime access is attached to the email address used during purchase. It is intended to give continued access without recurring subscription fees. It remains valid while The Patternizer service is active, the website is available, and the registered email account can be used for authentication. If the website permanently closes, the product is discontinued, or the email account is lost, deleted, inaccessible, or unable to authenticate, access may no longer be available.

This keeps the promise fair: you pay once, avoid monthly fees, and continue using the product as long as the service and your account remain operational.
